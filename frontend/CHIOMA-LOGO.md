# Chioma Brand Identity & Logo System

## Overview
This document introduces the official visual identity for **Chioma**, an open-source rental platform built on the Stellar blockchain. 

The logo system is designed to communicate:
- **Trust and Transparency:** Reflecting Chioma's role as financial infrastructure.
- **Real-world Connection:** Emphasizing housing and people-first use cases.
- **Scalability:** A versatile identity that works across borders, currencies, and platforms (web, mobile, wallets, and on-chain ecosystems).

The design aims to make Chioma feel trustworthy, modern, and truly Stellar-native, aligning with the mission to modernize rental transactions in emerging markets.

## Asset Overview & Features
- **Comprehensive Logo Suite:** Multiple variants (01-06) created to ensure flexibility across different backgrounds and sizes.
- **Vector & Raster Formats:** High-quality SVG (scalable) and PNG formats are provided for immediate use.
  - `Chioma Logo 01.svg` - `Chioma Logo 03.svg`
  - `Chioma Logo 04.png` & `.svg`
  - `Chioma Logo 05.png` & `.svg`
  - `Chioma Logo 06.svg`
- **Brand Consistency:** Utilizes a color palette that matches the web dApp and conveys financial stability.
- **Responsive Design:** Symbols and combination marks designed to scale from full brand usage to small protocol-level icons.

## Usage & Verification
To access and verify the logo assets:

1. **Navigate to the assets folder:**
   ```bash
   cd frontend/public/logo
   ```
2. **Visual Verification:**
   - Open the SVG and PNG files in a web browser or design tool (e.g., Figma, Illustrator).
   - Verify that the logos render clearly at both large and small sizes.
   - Check that the colors align with the existing dApp styling.
